#03 Human-Computer Interaction

Easy Map is an innovative digital solution designed to simplify emergency indoor navigation with a user-friendly interface. It addresses challenges such as complexity, poor design, and accessibility by offering essential features like a traditional map that displays crowd density to aid in evacuation planning, while the Augmented Reality map visually guides users to the nearest safe exit with directional indicators and sound notifications to ensure clarity during emergencies. Additionally, a "Contact Us" feature facilitates direct communication for assistance.

#04 Software Process Models

Bloom is a user-friendly desktop application that unites plant enthusiasts, sellers, and buyers on a single platform. Built with JavaFX, Bloom offers a dual-interface system for seamless plant sales and care management. Sellers can easily upload plant details and care instructions, while customers can browse plants and manage their collections. With integrated care management tools like watering reminders and care guides, Bloom simplifies plant care and fosters a thriving community of plant lovers. All data is stored and managed using MySQL, ensuring efficient data handling.

#05 Internet of Things

The IoT-based Laser Tag game integrates innovative technology for an interactive experience. Players register online and wear smart vests with laser sensors, RGB LEDs for hit feedback, and ultrasonic sensors for obstacle detection. Powered by an ESP32 microcontroller, the vest sends real-time hit data to the cloud via ThingSpeak, stores it in Firebase, and updates live scores and leaderboards on the website for detailed game results.
